Full Bathroom Service

Built Around The Whole Room

A full bathroom project can include shower tile, bathroom floor tile, waterproofing, vanity coordination, toilet replacement, mirror, lighting, hardware, grout, trim, and final cleanup.

What's Included

  • Demolition and preparation
  • Shower wall and shower floor tile
  • Bathroom floor tile
  • Waterproofing system
  • Niche, trim, grout, and finish details
  • Vanity, toilet, mirror, and fixture coordination
  • Daily cleanup

Average Timeline

Most bathroom projects take about one week on average, depending on size, tile layout, product availability, and scope.
