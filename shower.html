Shower FAQ

Do tiled showers need waterproofing?

Yes. Proper waterproofing is one of the most important parts of a long-lasting custom shower.

Can you add a niche or bench?

Yes. Niches, benches, shelves, curbs, and trim details can be planned into the shower layout.

Do you coordinate glass doors?

Yes. We can coordinate tile layout and finish details around glass door planning when needed.
