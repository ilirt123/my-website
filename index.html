Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Timeline depends on the size of the bathroom, the tile layout, product availability, and the scope of work. We review the schedule with you before work begins.

Do you install shower tile?

Yes. We install shower wall tile, shower floors, niches, curbs, benches, and trim details.

Do you handle waterproofing?

Yes. Waterproofing is part of a reliable shower and bathroom tile installation, and we use proven systems when the project calls for them.

Can you remodel a half bathroom?

Yes. We update powder rooms and half bathrooms with tile floors, accent walls, backsplashes, and finish details.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes. Contact All American Tiles to schedule a free estimate for your bathroom project.
